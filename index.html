Types Of Coffee

American Coffee

At the heart of what we do is a commitment to quality, sustainability, and community. We work closely with ethical producers to ensure that every cup you enjoy is not only delicious but also responsibly sourced. Whether you're looking for the perfect roast, the latest brewing equipment, or just some inspiration, we've got you covered.

Espresso

Espresso is a concentrated form of coffee served in small, strong shots. It's the foundation for many coffee drinks and is known for its rich flavor and intense caffeine kick. Our espresso is made from high-quality beans and is brewed to perfection, offering a bold and invigorating experience.

Latte

A latte is a creamy coffee drink made with espresso and steamed milk. It's a popular choice for those who enjoy a smoother, milkier coffee experience. Our lattes are crafted with care, ensuring a perfect balance of strong coffee flavor and silky milk texture.

Cappuccino

Cappuccino is a classic Italian coffee drink made with equal parts espresso, steamed milk, and frothed milk. It has a rich, bold flavor balanced with creamy, frothy milk. Perfect for those who enjoy a strong coffee with a velvety texture.

Macchiato

Macchiato is an espresso "stained" or "marked" with a small amount of steamed milk. It's a strong, bold coffee with just a hint of milk, ideal for those who prefer a more intense coffee flavor.

Mocha

Mocha combines rich espresso with chocolate syrup and steamed milk, topped with whipped cream. It's a delightful blend of coffee and chocolate for those who love a sweet and indulgent drink.

Cortado

Cortado is a Spanish coffee drink made by adding a small amount of warm milk to a shot of espresso. It offers a balanced coffee flavor with a creamy texture, ideal for those who enjoy a strong but slightly milder coffee experience.

Affogato

Affogato is a delicious Italian dessert that combines a shot of hot espresso poured over a scoop of vanilla ice cream. The contrast between the hot coffee and cold ice cream creates a delightful mix of flavors and textures.
